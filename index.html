Biography

Understanding security across global IoT supply chains and their real-world impact.

I study how security breaks across the global IoT supply chain from white-label manufacturing to cross-border marketplaces that distribute insecure devices at scale. My work focuses on safeguarding vulnerable user groups, including households and children, whose devices often inherit systemic weaknesses from opaque upstream vendors. I combine Internet-wide measurements, device and firmware reverse engineering, and machine learningbased analysis to uncover structural risks and translate empirical findings into actionable defenses.
